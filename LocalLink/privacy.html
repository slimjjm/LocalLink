Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

LocalLink ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the LocalLink app and website.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect information such as your email address and account identifiers. Authentication is handled securely using Firebase Authentication.

Booking Information

When customers make bookings, we store booking details such as date, time, service, and the associated business.

Future User Content

In future versions of LocalLink, users may be able to upload text or images (such as profile photos or reviews). If enabled, this content will be stored securely and only used to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the LocalLink platform
  • To manage user accounts and bookings
  • To communicate important service-related information
  • To improve the app and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase services (including Firestore and Authentication) to securely store and manage data. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information.

4. Payments

Payments are currently handled outside of the LocalLink app. LocalLink does not store or process payment card details at this time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is only shared where necessary to operate the service or where required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
